Yummy Candied Yams

Description

This recipe is for all my soul food lovers. Coming from enjoying soul food at least once a week in my household, I feel it's only right to have a soul-food recipe in the recipe book. If you've never tried candied yams I hope you try it, I'm sure you'll like it.

These yams coated in thick syrup are prefect for the holidays, sweet and delightful. Top with a dash of cinnamon if you like!

Ingredients

You got this. This one is easy!

  • 4 sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups white sugar

Directions

Direction, not directions. Just one step please don't let me down

Step 1

In a large pot over medium heat, place potatoes in water. Top with butter and sugar, cover and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer, without stirring, until mixture is thick and syrupy, 40 minutes. Mixture will thicken slightly as it cools. Remove from heat and serve warm.
