Fluffy Microwave Scrambled Eggs

Description

We all love eggs. At least most of us do. However, taking the time to get out your shiny nonstick pan, butter, eggs and trying to impress your non-existent significant other is hard. That's why I present this totally delicious definitely not lazy, Microwave Scrambled Eggs Recipe

With this recipe you can impress your crush or your parents by making eggs in under 5 minutes.

Ingredients

Ingredients!? Oh, you thought it was just eggs silly?

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/8 teaspoon of salt

Directions

Here's a overview of the steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Break eggs into a microwave-proof mixing bowl.
  3. Pop the bowl into the microwave and cook on high power for 30 seconds. Remove bowl, beat eggs while, scraping down sides of the bowl, and return to the microwave another 30 seconds
  4. Repeat the pattern, stirring every 30 seconds for up to 2 1/2 minutes. Stop when eggs have the consistency you desire.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
