World's Best Lasagna

Description

This is a copy of the AllRecipes: World's Best Lasagna created by John Chandler. This recipe is one of the most viewed of all time.

With this recipe you can make this delicious lasagna that will be surely be worth the wait.

Ingredients

  • Meat: This meaty lasagna has Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with meat for flavor.
  • Tomato Products: A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add sweetness and flavor.
  • Spices and seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Store bought lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta cheese.
  • Egg: An egg helps blend the ricotta.

How to Make Lasagna Step-By-Step

Here's a overview of the steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How to Layer

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with remaining Parmesan.

How Long to Cook Lasagna

The lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for final 25. Important to let lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
